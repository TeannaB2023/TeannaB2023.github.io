About the Author

I am Teanna Barrett, a Freshman Computer Science major from Austin, Texas. Academically, I am very interested in Data Science, a field that essentially combines Statistics and Computer Science to effectively analyze data. More specifically, I want to explore how large amounts of data are affecting society today and how Big Data can reform the future. Outside of academics, I love attending musical concerts, watching television shows and listening to podcasts. I enjoy consuming media in all forms because I get to experience new perspectives and emotions. Finally, my ultimate interest is social justice. My parents, especially my mother who is a Public Health expert, have instilled a passion for justice in my heart. I want everyone to have their humanity respected by the systems that rule society. When specific populations or individuals are disrespected, I highlight instances of injustice and search for solutions. All in all, I am an open-minded, human-centered, and inquisitive young woman who is trying to make the world better.
